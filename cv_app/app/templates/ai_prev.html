Gym Exercise Recognition

This program performs multi-label classification of images showing people working out at the gym. It identifies both the equipment being used and the specific exercise being performed.

Equipment Classification:

  • Machine
  • Body weight
  • Barbell
  • Dumbbell

Exercise Classification:

  • Squat
  • Lateral raise
  • Bench press
  • Bicep curl
  • Pull-up
  • Leg press
  • Pushup
